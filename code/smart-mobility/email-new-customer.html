Congratulations! You have completed the MetroBike training, and you are one step closer to claiming your free employee MetroBike membership!

To sign up for the City of Austin membership using your COA email:

  1. Go to https://austin.bcycle.com/sign-up-login
  2. Choose the "Local365" membership
  3. Click "Have a promo code?" and enter promo code: {PROMO_CODE}
  4. Enter your @austintexas.gov and @austinenergy.com "Email address", and click "Apply"
  5. Click "Continue" and complete your profile
  6. Enter your credit card info
    • Although your membership fee is waived in full, note that any usage charges (overages, damage to the bikes, etc.) will be charged to this card as outlined in the Terms and Conditions.
  7. Confirm the details and click "Finish"

If you run into any issues or have questions please contact: bikesharebenefit@austintexas.gov
